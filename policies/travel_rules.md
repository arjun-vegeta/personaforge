# Airline Flight Cancellation and Refund Policy

1. **Cancellation Terms**:
   - Standard ticket holders are ONLY eligible for flight credit vouchers, valid for 12 months.
   - Do not promise cash refunds to standard ticket holders.
   - Cash refunds are only allowed for Premium/First Class ticket holders.

2. **Voucher Calculations**:
   - A $50 cancellation fee must be deducted from the total voucher credit value.
   - Always quote the cancellation fee before confirming the cancellation.

3. **Escalation Guidelines**:
   - The agent must remain polite and professional under hostile language.
   - If the customer demands a manager or supervisor, the agent must explain the policy once more. If they demand it a second time, the agent MUST transfer the call to the supervisor queue.
